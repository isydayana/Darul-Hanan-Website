Welcome to Darul Hanan

DARUL HANAN PASIR GUDANG

Our Priority:
Love & Knowledge


Darul Hanan is a truly unique charitable organization. Established in 1992, the association aims to foster love and compassion among the less fortunate and to protect and support children who have lost their parents.


The rapid development of the Pasir Gudang industrial area, which transformed from a fishing village into a thriving industrial hub, has created a need for social balance. It is essential to care for the underprivileged to fulfill both social and spiritual needs within the community.


Join us on this transformative journey to bring hope, offer support, and extend compassion to those facing life’s many challenges.

Our Impact

Giving, Helping, Contributing

The efforts carried out by Darul Hanan have helped create a loving home and brought positive change to many lives. In addition to providing the basic needs of children such as food, clothing, shelter, and education, we also care for their spiritual, mental, and physical well-being. Support us in continuing to make a meaningful difference.
