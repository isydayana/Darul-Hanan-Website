Activities at Darul Hanan

We believe in building a well-rounded future for our children. Through various educational, recreational, and spiritual activities, we aim to instil values, skills, and confidence in every child.

Educational trips to museums and nature parks.

Sport

Sport for the soul where every child plays, learn, and grows.

Other Programs

Developing various life and practical skills through engaging activities.
